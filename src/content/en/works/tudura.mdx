I developed a simple browser game themed around filling in missing parts of mathematical equations. Despite its simplicity, it offers engaging gameplay.
As a Teaching Assistant, I coordinated ideas, made development decisions, provided coding advice, and implemented complex parts to help underclassmen lead the development.

You can play at https://mushikui.trasta.dev/ - give it a try and share it with your friends!
